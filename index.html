About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Dina Mariati, a passionate graphic designer with over 3 years of experience in the world of visual communication. I specialize in creating eye-catching flyers, but my journey goes beyond that. I’ve had the privilege of working on diverse design projects across both digital and print media, including social media graphics, brand identities, and promotional materials.

My Design Philosophy

For me, design is not just about making things look good—it's about telling a story. I focus on crafting designs that speak to the audience, making complex ideas simple and clear. By blending creativity with strategic thinking, I ensure every project I work on resonates with its intended message while staying true to the client’s vision.

What Drives Me

What drives me is the excitement of transforming ideas into visual concepts that engage and inspire. Whether it's designing a new logo, revamping a brand, or creating content that stands out on social media, I’m dedicated to delivering exceptional results that not only meet but exceed expectations.

Let’s Work Together!

Let’s collaborate and bring your vision to life through impactful and meaningful design!
